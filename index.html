‚ùì FAQ

What does the confidence level mean?

The confidence level shows how accurate we believe the text extraction is. Green (90-100%) means high confidence - the text should be very accurate. Red (below 90%) means you should double-check the results as some non-article content might be included.

Does this work on all websites?

Textify works on most article and blog websites using advanced extraction techniques including JSON-LD data, semantic HTML analysis, and intelligent content scoring. It's designed to handle modern news sites, blogs, and content management systems.

Is my data safe?

Yes! Textify only extracts text from the current page you're viewing. Nothing is sent to any server - all processing happens locally in your browser.

How is the text formatted for spreadsheets?

The extracted text is automatically cleaned to remove extra spaces, line breaks, and special characters, then formatted as a single line perfect for pasting into Google Sheets or Excel without expanding cell width.

What browsers are supported?

Textify works in all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
